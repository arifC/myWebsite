About Me

I was born in 1994 in Donaueschingen in Southern Germany. My family originally comes from Turkey and lives here since 1974 when my grandfather migrated here to work as a "Gastarbeiter". Beside my Bachelor's degree in Business Information System I've voluntarily worked as a certified paramedic for over 3 years. I'm interested in different topics like investment, emergency medicine, sociopolitical engagement and (obviously) computer science. In my leisure time I do sports (BJJ and fitness), read articles from geniuses like Martin Fowler or Jez Humble and engage in voluntary projects. Since 2015 I'm funded by the talent program of the Friedrich-Ebert-Stiftung where I'm a member of the internal working group "Internet".
In terms of informatics I'm really enthusiastic for the topics Continuous Integration/Delivery, Microservices, SAP Hybris and startups.
Check out my blog where I write about these and other points.
